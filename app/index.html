  • Urbbi is a prototype product-service concept that explores how to help people take care of the plants in their home while encouraging learning and sharing about how to grow their own food.   • Envelop is a software concept that allows you to create personal letters by printing customised stationary, giving you control over elements of the layout such as line height and images from your hard drive. Once you’ve created your layout it’s as simple as selecting the preferred postage and Envelop will print the stamp right onto your letter.
